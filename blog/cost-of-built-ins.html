How much do custom built-ins cost in Ontario?

A practical range guide for homeowners. Replace with your real pricing approach and examples.

Typical range: $2,000 – $8,000 for many built-ins, depending on size, finish, and hardware.

What drives price?

  • Size & complexity: full wall vs. a small unit
  • Materials: plywood, solid wood, veneer, paint-grade
  • Finish: paint, stain, lacquer
  • Hardware: soft-close hinges, slides, specialty fittings
  • Installation conditions: walls/levels, access, electrical/AV needs

How to get a fast estimate

  1. Send photos of the area
  2. Share rough dimensions
  3. Provide 1–2 inspiration photos
  4. Tell us your budget range and timeline
