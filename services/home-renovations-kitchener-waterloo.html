Home Renovations in Kitchener-Waterloo

Full-service home remodeling by KD Ltd. From single-room upgrades to whole-house transformations — 40+ years of expert craftsmanship.

Looking for a trusted home improvement company in Kitchener-Waterloo? KD Ltd has been helping homeowners across the KW region transform their homes for over four decades. Whether you need a kitchen overhaul, bathroom remodel, basement finishing, or a complete house renovation, we handle every aspect of the project from design through final walkthrough.

We're not a franchise or a handyman service — we're experienced tradespeople who specialize in custom carpentry and high-quality renovation work. Every project gets the same attention to detail, whether it's a single room makeover or a multi-room remodel.

Our Home Renovation Services

  • Kitchen Renovations — Custom cabinetry, countertops, layout redesigns, and complete kitchen overhauls.
  • Bathroom Remodeling — Full bathroom builds, vanity replacements, tile work, and spa-inspired upgrades.
  • Basement Finishing — Transform unfinished basements into living spaces, rec rooms, home offices, or rental suites.
  • Custom Cabinetry — Built-in shelving, entertainment centres, mudroom storage, and bespoke cabinetry designed for your space.
  • Home Repairs — Structural repairs, drywall, flooring, trim, doors, and general maintenance.
  • Whole-House Remodeling — Coordinated multi-room renovations that modernize your entire home while you stay on budget.

How Our Renovation Process Works

  1. Free consultation and estimate. We visit your home, discuss your vision, assess the space, and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote.
  2. Design and planning. We finalize the scope, select materials, and create a project timeline. Permits are pulled where required.
  3. Expert construction. Our team handles demolition, framing, electrical, plumbing, custom carpentry, and all finishing work.
  4. Final walkthrough. We walk through the completed project with you to make sure every detail meets your expectations before we consider the job done.
